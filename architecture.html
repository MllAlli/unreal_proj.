Epic Games offers two different solutions for real-time architectural visualization.

Unreal Engine

For visualization specialists

Unreal Engine is a complete suite of creation tools for photoreal visuals and real-time immersive experiences. With uncompromised quality, powerful performance, and proven results, Unreal Engine is the flexible, open platform visualization specialists can rely on to take their asset anywhere.

Twinmotion

For architects and designers

Quickly and easily produce high-quality images, panoramas, and standard or 360° VR videos in seconds! For architecture, construction, urban planning, and landscaping professionals, Twinmotion combines an intuitive icon-driven interface with the power of Unreal Engine.
