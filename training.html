Key features

Efficient data processing that fits your pipeline

Unreal Engine can fit in your existing pipeline, offering native support for many industry-standard 3D asset formats, virtual terrains, and communication protocols. Convert entire scenes at high fidelity, automate optimization processes like decimation and LODs with Python scripting, and easily create interactions with the Variant Manager and Blueprint visual scripting.

Create, manage, and stream large terrain databases

Unreal Engine’s World Composition feature enables you to manage the streaming of large terrain databases in order to optimize memory usage, by dividing them into individual tiles that can be loaded and unloaded automatically based on their distance from the viewer. You can create terrains from scratch using the built-in Landscape tool, or import a heightmap created in external tools.

Multi-platform deployment

From a single Unreal Engine project, you can deploy your simulation to a vast array of platforms, from mobile devices to touchscreens, VR headsets, and CAVEs. Pixel Streaming can remotely deliver high-performance interactivity on any device with a web browser, while the nDisplay system enables you to render real-time content at any resolution, and to display it on multiple LED or projection screens.

The level of realism you need

From true-to-life photorealism to headless projects, Unreal Engine lets you balance the level of fidelity in your scene against your other requirements. With both physically based rasterized and hybrid ray-traced rendering, you can achieve the look you want, including dynamic soft shadows from area lights, true reflections, and HDR image-based skylights. Plus, create film-quality real-time effects with built-in particles, physics, and destruction.
